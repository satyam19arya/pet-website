About Us

From 2000 to 2020

Our story began when I moved to a new neighborhood and couldn't find a dog walker for my dog. The recommended dog walkers were either over booked or offered all day outings with off-leash walks, not ideal for my reactive, senior dog. I knew there was a need for leashed one-on-one walks. I also knew I wanted to build a service company that I would use and trust with my own furbaby. I believe that education is key.Before forming an LLC I closely studied the pet industry and worked directly with a pet business coach.
